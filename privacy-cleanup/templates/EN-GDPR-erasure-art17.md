(`dpo@`, `privacy@`).

**My requests:**

1. **Right of access (Art. 15):** Confirm whether you process my personal

2. **Right to erasure (Art. 17):** Erase all personal data concerning me.

   marketing or based on legitimate interests, I also **object** under

3. **Right to restriction (Art. 18):** Pending verification, restrict
   processing of my data.
4. **Notification (Art. 19) & third parties (Art. 17(2)):** Inform any
   recipients and any controllers to whom you disclosed or made the data

5. **Stop sale/sharing:** Cease any sale, sharing, or onward transfer of
   my personal data with immediate effect.

within one month** of receipt (Art. 12(3)). If you do not comply, I will
lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority (Art. 77) and
seek a judicial remedy (Art. 79).

Please confirm receipt in writing and, once completed, confirm the erasure.
