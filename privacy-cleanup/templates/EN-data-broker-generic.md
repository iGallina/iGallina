# Template — Generic data broker removal (multi-framework)

Use when you don't know the broker's jurisdiction, or for a mispublished
document/CSV. Cites CCPA, GDPR and LGPD so at least one applies. Send to the
privacy/abuse contact and, for a hosted file, also the site/host's takedown
channel.

**Subject:** Personal data removal & opt-out request — [COMPANY NAME]

---

To Whom It May Concern,

My name is **[FULL NAME]**. I am writing to require the removal of my
personal data that **[COMPANY NAME]** holds, displays, processes, or sells.

**Data identifying me / the listing:**
- Name: [FULL NAME]
- Email(s): ian.gallina@gmail.com; redspell84@hotmail.com
- Exact location of my data (profile, listing, or file URL): [URL]
- Other identifiers shown: [phone / employer / address as displayed]

**I request that you, without undue delay:**

1. **Delete / erase** all personal data concerning me from your systems and
   any public-facing listing, page, search result, or file.
2. **Stop selling, sharing, or otherwise disclosing** my personal data to
   any third party, and instruct downstream recipients to delete it.
3. **Suppress** my information so it is not re-added by future data imports.
4. Disclose the **source** from which you obtained my data.
5. Confirm completion in writing.

**Legal basis (at least one applies):**
- **GDPR** (Reg. EU 2016/679) Art. 17 — right to erasure; Art. 21 —
  objection; respond within one month (Art. 12(3)).
- **CCPA/CPRA** (Cal. Civ. Code §1798.100 et seq.) §1798.105 — deletion;
  §1798.120 — opt-out of sale/sharing; respond within 45 days.
- **LGPD** (Brazil, Law 13.709/2018) Art. 18, VI — elimination; Art. 8 §5 —
  withdrawal of consent; I am a Brazil-based data subject.

If this concerns a **publicly hosted document/file** containing my data
without lawful basis, treat this also as a takedown request and remove or
redact the file; if you merely host it, please forward this to the
publisher and de-index it.

**Identity:** verified via the email(s) above; request only the minimum
additional data needed, via a secure channel.

Absent a timely response I will escalate to the relevant authority (ANPD /
the competent EU supervisory authority / the California Privacy Protection
Agency) and request search-engine de-indexing.

Regards,
**[FULL NAME]**
[CONTACT EMAIL] · [DATE]
